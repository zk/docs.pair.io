FAQ

Is pair.io for me?

Do you use vi, emacs, or other terminal-friendly editor? Are you comfortable on the command line? Do you write software? Then yes, this thing is for you.

What's pair.io about?

  1. Repeatable, disposable dev environments.
  2. Real-time, collaborative software development.
  3. Making 1 & 2 easy.

So why is it called pair.io?

I like the name pair.io, and it has something to do with what this is about. Also, dev.io was taken.
